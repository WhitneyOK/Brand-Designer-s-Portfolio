1845 -
baseball inspired apparel brand

1845 is a clothing brand that bridges the gap between baseball heritage and modern streetwear. Creating apparel inspired by the diamond, reimagined for the concrete jungle. Empowering individuals to express themselves through unique designs and high-quality materials, fostering a community of passionate hustlers who play by their own rules.

THE CHALLENGE

1845, a young brand trying to establish itself in the crowded streetwear market. They needed a way to differentiate themselves and connect with a passionate audience of baseball-loving individuals who also embrace urban street style.

1845 isn't just a brand, it is a celebration of passion and creativity that transcends the boundaries of traditional sportswear and the love of baseball. This brand celebrates creativity and expression through the love of baseball and style. 1845 is about redefining fandom, pushing boundaries, and celebrating the hustle that lives in each of us, beyond the bleachers. It is more than just a brand, it’s a movement.
